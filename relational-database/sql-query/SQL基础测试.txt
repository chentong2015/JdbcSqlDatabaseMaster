> select cat_id, count(order_id) as sales
  from t_tab
  where cat_id <> "c666"
  group by cat_id
  having count(order_id) > 10
  order by count(order_id) desc
  limit 100;
1. FROM先从表中筛选并过滤数据，再分组数据
2. Having操作(筛选)分组后的结果
3. 最后将分组结果排序和提取
